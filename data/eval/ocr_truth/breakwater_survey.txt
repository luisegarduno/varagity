Halcyon Breakwater Survey

The Halcyon Breakwater shelters the ferry terminal at Port Essing. It is 1,340 meters long, was completed in 2011, and its crest sits 4.6 meters above the mean high-water mark. The seaward face is armored with interlocking concrete anchor blocks cast on site.

The following page reproduces the dive team's scanned field note from the annual underwater inspection of the structure.

DIVERS COUNTED FIFTY EIGHT ANCHOR BLOCKS ALONG THE NORTH FACE OF THE HALCYON BREAKWATER.
